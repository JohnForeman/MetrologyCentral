# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: February 7, 2026_

Metrology Central (“we,” “our,” or “this site”) is committed to respecting your privacy.
This page explains what data may be collected when you use this website and how it is used.

## 1) What data we collect

### Information you provide directly

If you contact us by email, we may receive:

- your name,
- your email address,
- and the message content you choose to send.

We only use this information to respond to your inquiry and related follow-up.

### Information collected automatically

Like most websites, we may collect basic technical data automatically, such as:

- browser type,
- device type,
- pages visited,
- referring links,
- approximate location by IP,
- and date/time of visits.

This data helps us understand site usage and improve content quality.

## 2) Cookies and tracking

This site may use cookies or similar technologies for:

- website functionality,
- analytics,
- and advertising services.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

## 3) Advertising and AdSense

Metrology Central may display ads through Google AdSense.
Google and its partners may use cookies to show ads based on your visits to this and other sites.

To learn more or control ad personalization, visit:

- <https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads>
- <https://adssettings.google.com/>

## 4) Analytics

We may use analytics services to understand traffic and content performance.
Analytics data is used in aggregate to improve articles, navigation, and user experience.

## 5) How we use information

Information collected is used to:

- maintain and improve the site,
- respond to messages,
- monitor performance and reliability,
- and support content and advertising operations.

We do not sell personal information.

## 6) Data sharing

We may share limited data with service providers that help run the site (for example, hosting, analytics, and ad delivery providers), only as needed for those services.

## 7) Data retention

We retain data only as long as reasonably necessary for operational, legal, or security purposes.

## 8) Third-party links

This site may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies before sharing information.

## 9) Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect data, but no internet transmission or storage method is 100% secure.

## 10) Children’s privacy

This site is intended for professional audiences and is not directed to children under 13.

## 11) Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding access, correction, or deletion of personal data. Contact us to make a request.

## 12) Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact:

- **Email:** <a href="mailto:contact@metrologycentral.com">contact@metrologycentral.com</a>

## 13) Policy updates

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the current version.

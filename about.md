# About Metrology Central

**Metrology Central** is a knowledge-sharing platform dedicated to dimensional metrology, quality systems, and measurement technologies used in manufacturing environments.

## Our Mission

We aim to provide practical, experience-based insights that help professionals working with:

- Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMMs)
- Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T)
- Model-Based Definition (MBD) and Quality Information Framework (QIF) workflows
- Production quality systems
- Precision measurement tools and techniques

## What We Cover

This site serves as a resource hub for:

- **Engineers** implementing quality systems and digital workflows
- **Inspectors and Technicians** working with measurement equipment
- **Programmers** developing inspection software and automation
- **Quality Professionals** managing quality assurance processes
- **Manufacturing Professionals** interested in metrology and quality

## Content Focus

Our content covers:

- Technical tutorials and best practices
- Real-world case studies and lessons learned
- Tool and technology reviews
- Industry standards and methodologies
- Digital transformation in quality departments
- Emerging technologies in metrology

## Contact

This site is maintained as an open knowledge resource. For questions, suggestions, or contributions, please reach out through the repository's issue tracker.
